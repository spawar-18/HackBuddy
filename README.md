# HackBuddy

### Your AI-Powered Hackathon Companion

**Stop juggling tabs. Start winning hackathons.**

HackBuddy is an AI-powered workspace that acts as your team's **Hackathon assistant and AI Mentor** — all in one platform. From brainstorming an idea to the final project submission, HackBuddy keeps teams organized, aligned, and productive throughout the hackathon.

---

# 🚨 The Problem

Hackathons are fast-paced, and teams often struggle with coordination rather than coding.

Common challenges include:

- Scattered communication across multiple tools
- Unclear task ownership
- Poor project planning
- Lack of strategic feedback
- Difficulty tracking overall progress

Using different platforms for chatting, planning, tracking, and documentation often leads to confusion, duplicated work, and missed deadlines.

---

# 💡 The Solution

HackBuddy brings everything into a single AI-powered workspace.

It helps teams:

- Plan projects
- Split tasks intelligently
- Collaborate efficiently
- Monitor development
- Receive AI-powered guidance throughout the hackathon

Instead of switching between multiple tools, teams manage their entire workflow from one dashboard.
